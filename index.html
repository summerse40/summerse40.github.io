Hello, I'm Emily!

            
Web developer, Data Analyst, artist, and avid hiker. With over 3 years in the tech industry and a recent graduate from my master in science, I combine my love for design and coding to create user-friendly experiences and to formulate insights
                based on data.

        



    


    

        


            

                
 I recently graduated with my master in science of computer science in May 2023, and I am eager to join the workforce after recently completing a temporary job within my field domain. Previous experiences include big data analytics and
                    machine learning research throughout my graduate degree and experience freelancing in website development for small business owners. 

 I come from a bit of an odd background to be in computer science since my Bachelor of Science
                    is in biomedical sciences. My first introduction to computer science wasn’t until the end of my undergraduate degree before I realized the power of technology and the influence it can have for the greater good. I hope to achieve a
                    job working within computer science that may leverage my experiences within the science laboratory and clinical endeavors. 

 In addition to work, I value friendships and family and am a big nature enthusiast who is an amateur
                    birder in the Northwestern Florida panhandle and who doesn’t mind kicking up some dust. Thank you for taking the time to review my landing page. Please feel free to reach out with any questions you may have or improvements you would
                    recommend to my application. Thank you!
